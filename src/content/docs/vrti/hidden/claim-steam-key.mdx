If you purchased **VRTI** through **LemonSqueezy** before the Steam release, you can claim a free Steam key here.
Thank you for your early support and for taking a moment to migrate your copy. 

To claim the key:

1. Enter your original **VRTI license key**
2. Enter either the **purchase email address** or the **LemonSqueezy order ID**
3. Submit the form to receive your Steam key

The non-Steam version of VRTI will continue to work as normal for the foreseeable future, but it will no longer receive feature updates.

You can activate your Steam key on the [Steam website](https://store.steampowered.com/account/registerkey) or through the Steam client.

VRTI automatically tracks your usage over time, allowing you to view your historic statistics and progress.
Historic data is only ever stored locally on your computer.

<center>
  <img src={HistoricStatsImage.src} alt="Historic Statistics" class="rounded" />
</center>

### Tracking Points

VRTI records every session you have with your treadmill, tracking the following metrics:

- **Active Time:** The total time you spent walking or running.
- **Distance:** The total distance you covered.
- **Steps:** The number of steps you took (if supported by your treadmill).

### Viewing Statistics

You can view your statistics in different ways to analyze your progress:

- **Weekly View:** Shows a daily breakdown of your stats for a selected week.
- **Yearly View:** Shows a monthly breakdown of your stats for a selected year.
